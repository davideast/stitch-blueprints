Typography is the voice. The design takes its content as seriously as a great newspaper takes its reporting.

Dense but readable. Every typographic decision references the tradition of long-form print — column grids, bylines, datelines, pull quotes. Headlines command. Body copy earns sustained reading. Labels carry precise metadata. The interface does not perform — it informs.

Editorial rejects: centered section headings, decorative illustration that competes with content, warm or playful color palettes, rounded UI conventions, visual clutter that dilutes the written word.
