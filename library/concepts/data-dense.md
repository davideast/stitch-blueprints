A high-precision instrument for the technical observer. Not a consumer product — a professional tool.

Every element is accountable to function. The grid is strict and engineering-focused. Intentional asymmetry, high-contrast tonal depth, zero decorative noise. Information density is not a problem to solve — it is the design intent. Whitespace is rationed, not given freely. The user came here to work.

Monospace type for data. Labels everywhere. No ambiguity about what any number means or where it came from. The aesthetic marries the density of a financial terminal with the precision of a luxury instrument.

Data dense rejects: marketing whitespace, hero sections, decorative illustration, centered alignment, anything that prioritizes feeling over function.
