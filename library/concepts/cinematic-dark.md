A space of total control. Light is precise and purposeful — it appears only where it needs to be seen.

The canvas is near-black. Elements emerge from shadow only when they have something to say. Color is used with surgical restraint: one vivid accent against an achromatic field. The mood is focused, professional, and cinematic. The design does not try to be friendly — it tries to be exact.

Cinematic dark rejects: warm backgrounds, multiple accent colors, decorative animation, anything that draws attention without earning it. Every element must justify its luminosity.
