name: "Context Menu"
slot: ui-component
type: structural
tags: [right-click, popover, actions, overflow-menu]
---

A floating list of actions that appears anchored to a trigger — right-click, long-press, or an overflow (⋯) button. Items are a single action label with an optional leading icon. Destructive actions appear at the bottom, separated by a divider, and are styled in error color. The menu dismisses on outside click or any selection.
