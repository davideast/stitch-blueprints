A single node in a timeline — a dot or icon on a vertical spine, connected to a content block. The content block contains a timestamp, a title, and an optional body with supporting detail, attachments, or metadata. Entries may be expandable to show more detail. The connector line between nodes is styled to indicate sequence — solid for completed, dashed for pending.
