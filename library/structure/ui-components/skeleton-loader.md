Placeholder shapes that mirror the layout of content while it loads. Rectangles and circles in muted surface tones with a subtle shimmer animation. Skeletons match the exact dimensions of the real content they replace — headline blocks, avatar circles, body text lines. No spinner; the shape itself communicates what is coming.
