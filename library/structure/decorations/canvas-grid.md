Decorative border grid applied across the full page. Horizontal lines extend edge-to-edge at full viewport width. Vertical lines remain within the page container. Creates a structural grid framework that makes the page feel architected rather than templated — without requiring any custom graphics.

Inspired by Stripe, Adio, and Tailwind. Apply as a finishing layer at low contrast. Pairs best with sharp (0px) roundedness and editorial or data-dense layouts.
