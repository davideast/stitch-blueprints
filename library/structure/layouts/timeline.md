name: "Timeline"
slot: layout
type: structural
tags: [chronological, history, progress, vertical-sequence]
---

Events or steps arranged along a vertical spine — a line connecting nodes in chronological or sequential order. Each node has a timestamp, title, and supporting detail. The spine can be centered (alternating left/right entries) or left-aligned (all entries to the right). Used for history, progress tracking, changelogs, and process flows.
