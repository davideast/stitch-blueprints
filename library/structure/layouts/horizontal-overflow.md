Content is organized in horizontal scrolling rows — shelves of cards, each row a category. The page scrolls vertically between shelves; each shelf scrolls horizontally through its items. Creates a sense of abundance without overwhelming vertical length. Common in streaming, e-commerce, and content discovery apps.
