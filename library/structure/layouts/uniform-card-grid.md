  A responsive grid of equal-size cards arranged in rows and columns. Card count per row adapts to viewport width (typically 4 on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile). The most common collection view in software — products, articles, team members, projects, files. Choose this when items are peers with no inherent hierarchy beyond sort order. Unlike masonry, uniform cards signal equal importance across all items. Pairs with almost any aesthetic; the card design carries the visual identity. Works for catalogs, search results, portfolios, and dashboards.
