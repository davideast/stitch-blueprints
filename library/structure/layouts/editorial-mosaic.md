A non-uniform grid where featured items occupy large cells and secondary items fill smaller adjacent cells. Inspired by newspaper front pages and magazine spreads — the hierarchy is spatial, not just typographic. One or two items dominate; everything else supports. The composition changes per section, creating a sense of editorial curation rather than templated repetition.
