The standard shell for marketing sites, documentation, and lightweight web apps. Choose this when content needs maximum horizontal width and navigation is secondary to the content itself. Simpler and less space-hungry than a sidebar shell. Works best with fewer than eight top-level nav items. Pairs well with hero-full-screen, single-column-article, and uniform-card-grid as inner content patterns.
