  Two persistent panels side by side: the left panel (280–360px) contains a scrollable list of items — each row shows a thumbnail, title, and metadata. Selecting a row loads the full detail view in the right panel without a page navigation. On mobile, the two panels stack and navigate as separate screens. The selected list item is visually highlighted.

The master-detail pattern — used by email clients, file browsers, settings pages, CRMs, and any app where users browse a list and inspect individual items. Choose this when users frequently switch between items without losing their place in the list. Dramatically reduces navigation overhead compared to a drill-down pattern. Pairs with compact list typography and a subtle selection highlight color.
