  A header zone spans full width: a banner image or gradient fills the top (180–240px tall); the user avatar overlaps the bottom of the banner, anchored left or center; name, handle, and bio sit adjacent or below the avatar. Below the header, a tab bar or section anchors switch between content areas (posts, about, media, connections). Selected tab content fills the remaining page width.

The identity and portfolio layout for social platforms, professional networks, and community apps. Choose this when a person, brand, or entity is the primary subject of the page. The banner-plus-avatar header pattern is widely recognized and sets immediate context. Tab-switching keeps the profile header persistent while varying the content below. Pairs with any aesthetic; the user's content and avatar carry the visual identity.
