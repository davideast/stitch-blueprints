  Arrange content in alternating full-width horizontal sections — image left/text right,

The classic marketing page structure: alternating image-and-text strips that walk the reader through a series of features or benefits. Choose this for product pages, landing pages, or any context where you need to present multiple value propositions in a scannable, predictable rhythm. Pairs well with generous whitespace and bold headlines.
