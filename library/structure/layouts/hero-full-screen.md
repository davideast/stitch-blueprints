A full-viewport hero that dedicates the entire first screen to a single statement. Choose this when the product or message deserves an uninterrupted first impression — nothing competes above the fold. Expresses minimalism through radical focus: one element per role, nothing extraneous. Pairs with editorial-serif for dramatic headlines and tonal-layering for flat backgrounds.
