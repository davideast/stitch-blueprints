The product detail page — the most conversion-critical layout in ecommerce. Choose this when users need to inspect a product visually while making a purchase decision. The two-zone above-fold structure keeps imagery and action in constant view. Pairs with a clean, low-distraction aesthetic to keep focus on the product. Works for physical goods, digital products, and subscription plans.
