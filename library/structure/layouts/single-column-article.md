The canonical reading layout. Choose this for blog posts, essays, documentation articles, or any longform content where sustained reading is the goal. The narrow column forces comfortable line lengths (65–80 characters). Pairs exceptionally well with editorial-serif or mono-grotesque typography specs and a minimal or tonal color scheme. Keep distractions out — no sidebars, no floating nav.
