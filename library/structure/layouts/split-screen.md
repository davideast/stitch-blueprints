A two-panel layout that divides the viewport into distinct halves. Choose this when the content naturally has two sides — comparison, dual entry points, or a media/content split (like login screens with imagery on one side). The split itself is the structural statement. More general-purpose than other minimalist layouts; works across many aesthetics. Pairs with any typography spec and any elevation system.
