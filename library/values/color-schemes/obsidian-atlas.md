## Color Roles

- **Primary** (`#0A0E1A`): Near-black navy ink — HSL(225°, 46%, 8%). All text, headings, core content. Near-black with strong blue undertone — references archival ink on dark stock.
- **Secondary** (`#2C3A4A`): Deep slate — supporting structure, borders, captions. Mid-dark blue-gray that bridges the obsidian canvas and the near-black primary.
- **Tertiary** (`#C9A227`): Vivid antique gold — HSL(43°, 65%, 47%). The single accent: CTAs, the compass rose, the coordinate marker. S=65%, L=47% — valid Tertiary, lands cleanly.
- **Neutral** (`#080C14`): Obsidian canvas — HSL(225°, 46%, 6%). The dark ground. Near-absolute black with a blue-black hue — deeper than the Primary, creating the tonal depth that elevates panels.

## Notes

Dark mode where both Primary and Neutral are near-black — the 2% luminance differential (L 8% vs L 6%) creates depth. The gold Tertiary is the only warmth in an entirely cold palette.
