## Color Roles

- **Primary** (`#1B3026`): Deep midnight forest green — near-black with strong green hue. The infield at dusk. Maximum contrast against the cream canvas. All text, headings, and core content.
- **Secondary** (`#7A6A5A`): Warm weathered slate — old concrete, worn rubber, aged canvas. Supporting captions, borders, metadata.
- **Tertiary** (`#2471A3`): Vivid athletic blue — the Potomac at dawn, the DC sky over the back straight, the race bib number. HSL ~205°, S 64%, L 39%. The single vivid accent: CTAs, emphasis, the lane marker.
- **Neutral** (`#F2ECE2`): Warm cream — archival paper, vintage athletic programme, linen. The canvas.

## Notes

Evokes the natural and athletic world of a running track — forest green infield, the clear DC sky, warm cream of a printed race programme. Cool and collegiate without being cold. Blue is vivid enough (L 39%, S 64%) to land reliably in `tertiary` when Primary is near-black.
