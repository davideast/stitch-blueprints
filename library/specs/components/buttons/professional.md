  to equalize visual height.

Precise, production-grade button spec that eliminates common AI-generated button problems: oversized hit targets, icon clutter, and muddy border-shadow edges. Choose this when buttons need to feel clean and intentional. Pairs well with editorial-shadow elevation and left-aligned-editorial headlines.
