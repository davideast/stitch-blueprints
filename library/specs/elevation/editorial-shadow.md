---
name: "Editorial Shadow"
slot: elevation
type: influence
tags: [shadow, editorial, print-inspired, structured]
---

Depth is expressed through deliberate box shadows — not ambient blurs, but sharp or semi-sharp drop shadows that reference print design and physical layering. Cards cast hard shadows at low spread. Modals use a clear, contained shadow that separates them from the page. The shadow palette is derived from the primary color at low opacity, not pure black, keeping the depth warm and on-brand.
