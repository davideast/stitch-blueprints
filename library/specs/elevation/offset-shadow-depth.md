Analog print-inspired depth through hard-edged offset shadows — like die-cut cardstock on a table. Choose this for craft, risograph, or 2.5D aesthetics where elements should feel tangible and pressable. Pairs naturally with flat matte fills and sharp/zero roundedness. Mutually exclusive with blurred shadows and glassmorphism.
