Analog grain overlay that makes dark surfaces feel like physical material — photographic prints, screen-printed posters, darkroom output. Choose this for cinematic, esoteric, or dark-analog contexts where flat color reads as sterile. Apply to containers, not page canvas. Pairs with tonal-layering, glass-blur, and cinematic-dark concepts. Do not use on light backgrounds.
