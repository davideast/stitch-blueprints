A shadow-free elevation system that creates depth through graded surface colors. Choose this when you want a modern, flat aesthetic where hierarchy comes from tonal contrast rather than drop shadows or blur. Expresses minimalism's preference for restraint. Pairs naturally with ring-edge-definition for clean container boundaries, and with editorial-serif or humanist-sans for typographic-driven hierarchy.
