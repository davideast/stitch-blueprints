A warm, readable body type spec designed for sustained reading. Choose this when long-form content or explanatory text needs to feel human and approachable rather than clinical. Expresses minimalism through its quiet confidence — the type disappears into the reading experience. Pairs with editorial-serif for headlines and tonal-layering for flat elevation.
