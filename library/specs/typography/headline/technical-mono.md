Headlines use a monospaced typeface — uppercase, wide-tracked, treating text as a structured output rather than a fluid composition. The monospace convention signals precision and system-thinking. Pairs with a neutral sans-serif for body to create contrast between the "machine" headline layer and the "human" reading layer.
