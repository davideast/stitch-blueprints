A prestige headline treatment rooted in magazine and longform journalism typography. Choose this when the design needs a strong typographic anchor that commands attention without decorative elements. The serif becomes the primary visual personality — especially powerful in minimalist layouts where type does all the heavy lifting. Pairs with humanist-sans for body text and tonal-layering for shadow-free elevation.
