name: "Tonal Layering"
slot: elevation

tags: [flat, modern, no-shadows, material, depth-through-color]
---

This design uses no shadows. Depth is conveyed entirely through surface color variation — `surface-container-lowest` for recessed areas, `surface-container` for cards, `surface-container-high` for elevated panels. No drop shadows anywhere. The hierarchy is readable through tonal contrast alone.
