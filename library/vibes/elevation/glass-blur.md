Floating elements use frosted glass treatment — `backdrop-blur` with semi-transparent surface fills. Overlays, modals, and sticky headers are translucent, allowing the layer below to bleed through diffused. Depth is expressed through blur radius, not shadow spread. Reserved for hero sections and floating UI; base surfaces remain opaque.
