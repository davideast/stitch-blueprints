**Do** left-align hero sections with a split layout: large headline on the left (~3/5 width), description and CTA stacked on the right (~2/5 width), both top-aligned.

**Don't** center-align hero sections. Centered heroes are the single most recognizable AI-default pattern — they signal that no editorial judgment was applied to the layout.
