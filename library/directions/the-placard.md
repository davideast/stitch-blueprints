## When to use
Design needs institutional weight and formality. Content is a statement or announcement that should feel permanent and considered — like something mounted on a wall.

## What it moves
Centers content in a formal block with generous and equal margins, removes visual decoration, adopts announcement/institutional framing. References gallery placards, building dedications, formal program covers.
