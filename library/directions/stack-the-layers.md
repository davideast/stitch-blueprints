## When to use
When the design feels flat and everything sits on the same plane. Reach for this when depth and visual hierarchy need reinforcement through layering.

## What it moves
Adds box-shadows, z-index stacking, and overlapping positioned elements. Cards and panels shift from flush to elevated, creating foreground/midground/background relationships. Transforms a single-plane layout into a layered composition.
