"The timeline. Organize everything on a vertical spine. Time flows downward."

## When to use
Design has sequential or historical content being presented as a grid or card layout when the chronological relationship is the most important thing to communicate.

## What it moves
Introduces a vertical timeline spine, anchors content to dated or sequenced points along it, communicates progression and history through position rather than labels. References changelog pages, event histories, activity feeds.
