## When to use
When content floats in the middle or clusters at the top. Use this to create visual gravity — grounding content at the bottom while the upper viewport stays open and expansive.

## What it moves
Applies justify-content flex-end, margin-top auto, or absolute bottom positioning to primary content blocks. Content migrates from center or top to the bottom edge. Upper viewport becomes negative space, creating a grounded, editorial composition.
