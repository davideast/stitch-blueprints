## When to use
Design has accumulated decorative or contextual elements that are answering questions nobody asked. The core signal is obscured by surrounding noise.

## What it moves
Strips to the two essential questions: identity and action. Everything else is removed. Related to strip-to-essence but framed as a signal/noise filter rather than a stylistic reduction.
