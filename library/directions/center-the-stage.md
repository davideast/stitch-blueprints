## When to use
When the layout feels scattered or left-heavy. Use this to create a sense of poise and ceremony — everything presented on a single centered stage.

## What it moves
Shifts text-align to center, applies margin auto on containers, and sets flex/grid alignment to center. Left-aligned layouts become symmetrical. Content converges on the vertical midline, creating a formal, focused presentation axis.
