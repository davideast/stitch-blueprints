## When to use
When content flows continuously and sections blend together without clear breaks. Use this to create a presentation-like pacing where each section gets its own full-screen moment.

## What it moves
Applies min-height 100vh and flex centering to each major section. Content sections expand to fill the viewport. The scroll experience shifts from continuous flow to discrete, full-screen slides with clear section boundaries.
