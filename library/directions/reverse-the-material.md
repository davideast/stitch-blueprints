## When to use
When the design feels too predictable for its medium. Use this to create surprise and tension by making a website feel like a magazine, or a print-inspired layout feel like a native app.

## What it moves
Swaps font families (sans to serif or vice versa), adjusts color temperature (cool digital blues to warm paper tones or reverse), and changes texture treatments. The entire material language flips, creating a cross-medium aesthetic that feels unexpected.
