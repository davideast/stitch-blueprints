## When to use
Design has warm tones (amber, cream, warm white) that are softening a context that should feel precise, clinical, or astronomical.

## What it moves
Shifts all neutral tones toward cool blue-white or blue-black, removes amber and warm accent colors, introduces the temperature register of scientific instruments, medical interfaces, or deep-space data platforms.
