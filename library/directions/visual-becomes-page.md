"The supporting visual becomes the page. The content is now the caption."

## When to use
Image is decorative or contained when it should be dominant. Design is content-first when the visual carries more weight than the words.

## What it moves
Expands image to full viewport or panel dominance, reduces all copy to caption scale, inverts the content/visual relationship entirely.
