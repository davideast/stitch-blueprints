## When to use
Design needs the authority and density of print journalism. Too much whitespace is making content feel tentative rather than definitive.

## What it moves
Introduces narrow multi-column grid with print proportions, increases type density, references newspaper broadsheet layout — gutter-divided columns, editorial hierarchy across the grid.
