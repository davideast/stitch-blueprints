## When to use
Design leads with the headline without establishing context or category. The eyebrow/label exists but is too small to do its job as a framing device.

## What it moves
Establishes a prominent uppercase category label above the headline, creates a two-tier typographic hierarchy where context precedes statement, gives the overline enough scale to be read as a design element.
