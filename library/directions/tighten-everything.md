## When to use
When the design feels too airy or relaxed and needs urgency. Use this to compress the composition into a dense, high-information layout with kinetic tension.

## What it moves
Reduces margin, padding, gap, and line-height values across all elements. Whitespace shrinks dramatically. Elements crowd together, creating a dense typographic texture. The rhythm shifts from leisurely to urgent and compressed.
