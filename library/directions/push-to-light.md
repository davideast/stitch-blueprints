## When to use
When the design is too heavy or moody and needs to feel open and airy. Use this to shift the entire tonal range toward brightness and lightness.

## What it moves
Replaces dark background colors with white or near-white values. Reduces contrast ratios. Text shifts to medium grays on white. The overall palette compresses into the light end of the spectrum, creating an open, breathable canvas.
