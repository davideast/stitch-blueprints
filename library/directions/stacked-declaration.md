"Stacked declaration. Each phrase on its own line, different weight. The page is a statement."

## When to use
Design has a multi-part headline or value proposition being compressed into a single line. Each phrase has independent weight and deserves its own typographic moment.

## What it moves
Breaks multi-part headline into individual lines, assigns varying type weights or sizes to each phrase, creates a vertical typographic rhythm where each line lands before the next. References editorial covers, poster design, brand manifestos.
