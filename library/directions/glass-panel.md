## When to use
When content sits on heavy opaque containers that block the background. Use this to create a sense of transparency and atmospheric depth over rich imagery.

## What it moves
Applies backdrop-filter blur, semi-transparent background colors (rgba), and subtle border treatments. Containers shift from solid to translucent. Background imagery becomes visible through content panels, creating a glassy, layered atmosphere.
