## When to use
When the layout is too orderly and every element sits neatly in its box. Use this to create visual tension and editorial energy through intentional overlap and collision.

## What it moves
Applies negative margins, absolute/relative positioning offsets, and grid items that span beyond their tracks. Elements break out of their containers and overlap neighbors. The clean grid dissolves into a dynamic, layered editorial composition.
