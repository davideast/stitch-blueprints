## When to use
When the design has a clear identity but plays it safe. Use this to push the defining characteristic past comfortable into memorable — amplifying what already works.

## What it moves
Scales up the dominant visual property: font-size increases, color saturation rises, spacing exaggerates, or weight gets bolder. The strongest element becomes dramatically more prominent. The design's personality sharpens from subtle to unmistakable.
