## When to use
When repeated elements feel monotonous because they all sit on the same baseline. Use this to add visual rhythm and movement to lists, grids, and sequences.

## What it moves
Applies nth-child margin offsets, alternating padding, and transform translateY to repeated elements. Items shift off their shared baseline. Uniform grids become staggered compositions with a syncopated, editorial rhythm.
