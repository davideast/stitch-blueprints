## When to use
When the design feels sharp and rigid. Use this to shift the personality from corporate or technical toward friendly, modern, and approachable.

## What it moves
Increases border-radius across all containers, buttons, inputs, and image masks. Sharp 0px corners become large radii (16px–999px). Buttons shift from rectangular to pill-shaped. The overall feel moves from angular precision to soft warmth.
