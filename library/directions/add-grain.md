## When to use
When the design feels too clean and digitally perfect. Use this to add tactile warmth and the imperfect quality of physical media — film, newsprint, letterpress.

## What it moves
Adds SVG or CSS-generated noise overlays via pseudo-elements and background-image. Surfaces gain subtle granular texture. Images pick up film-grain effects. The overall feel shifts from pixel-perfect digital to textured analog, adding warmth and materiality.
