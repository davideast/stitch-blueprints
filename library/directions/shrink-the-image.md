## When to use
When images dominate the layout and leave no room for type or space to breathe. Use this when photography should support rather than lead the composition.

## What it moves
Shrinks image containers via max-width and max-height constraints. Increases surrounding margin and padding. Photography shifts from full-bleed hero to small, contained accent. Whitespace becomes the dominant visual element.
