"Make it feel like a physical artifact. Large imagery, sparse text, generous breathing room."

## When to use
Design feels digital and frictionless when it should feel considered and made. Too much text competing with imagery.

## What it moves
Expands imagery scale, reduces copy density, increases whitespace, shifts toward gallery or print publication rhythm.
