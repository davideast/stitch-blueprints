"Pull the most compelling sentence and make it the hero. Everything else is the article."

## When to use
Design leads with a generic headline when there's a specific, surprising, or human sentence buried in the body that deserves prominence.

## What it moves
Demotes the headline to secondary role, promotes a specific sentence — a user quote, a data statement, a provocative claim — to full display scale. References magazine pullquotes promoted to cover treatment.
