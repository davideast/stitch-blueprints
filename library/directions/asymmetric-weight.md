"Asymmetric weight. 70% of content mass on one side. Let the other breathe."

## When to use
Design is balanced in a way that reads as static or safe. Equal visual weight on both sides creates symmetry without tension.

## What it moves
Pushes content mass to one side (left or right), leaves the opposing side as negative space, creates compositional tension that feels deliberate and editorial rather than default-centered.
