"Data before narrative. Lead with the number at display scale. The explanation is the footnote."

## When to use
Design buries its most important metric beneath context-setting copy. The number is the point — make it the first thing seen.

## What it moves
Promotes a key metric to dominant scale, reduces surrounding copy to supporting role, treats the number as the primary typographic object.
