## When to use
When hero images feel small or lost in their containers. Use this when the subject needs to dominate the viewport and create immediate visual impact.

## What it moves
Changes object-fit, object-position, and container sizing to maximize image fill. Crops away negative space around subjects. Images shift from contextual to confrontational, filling their containers edge-to-edge.
