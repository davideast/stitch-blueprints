"Invert the palette. What was light goes dark. What was dark goes light."

## When to use
Design is strong but feels stuck in one mode. Exploring the inverse often reveals which version has more character.

## What it moves
Flips the luminance relationship of all surfaces — light canvas becomes dark canvas, dark type becomes light type. Accent colors may need adjustment to maintain contrast ratios.
