## When to use
Design is relying on imagery that isn't exceptional. The concept or headline is strong enough to stand alone. Typography can carry the full visual weight.

## What it moves
Removes imagery entirely, promotes headline type to full display scale, treats the typographic composition as the primary visual object. The layout becomes a poster.
