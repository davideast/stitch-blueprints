"The number. One metric at enormous scale, centered. Everything else is context."

## When to use
Design has a compelling metric that gets buried in surrounding content. The number is the argument — make it the entire screen.

## What it moves
Promotes a single key metric to full display scale, reduces all supporting content to footnote role, creates a monumental numerical statement. Related to flip-hierarchy but metric-specific.
