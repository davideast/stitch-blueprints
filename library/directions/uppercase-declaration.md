## When to use
Design tone is too conversational or approachable when it should be authoritative. Mixed-case type reads as friendly when the content demands weight.

## What it moves
Shifts all prominent type to uppercase, changes the register from conversational to declarative, introduces institutional or monumental character. Works best with condensed or tracked typefaces.
