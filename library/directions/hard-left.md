"Push everything hard left. Ragged right edge. The void is to the right."

## When to use
Design is centered or evenly distributed when it should feel directional and editorial. Centered layouts read as default; hard left reads as a decision.

## What it moves
Left-aligns all content without centering, allows content to end organically creating ragged right edge, uses right-side negative space as intentional compositional element.
